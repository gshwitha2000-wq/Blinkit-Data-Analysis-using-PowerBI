# 🛒 Blinkit Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **Blinkit Data Analysis Dashboard** is a Power BI project that provides comprehensive insights into product performance, outlet characteristics, and sales trends. It helps stakeholders understand customer preferences, optimize inventory, and improve business decisions using data-driven analytics.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📊 Business Summary

* **Total Sales:** 1.20M
* **Average Rating:** 3.92
* **Total Item Visibility:** 7,880
* **Maximum Item Weight:** 21.35

---

### ⭐ Product Insights

#### 📦 Rating by Item Type

* Categories include:

  * Fruits & Vegetables
  * Snack Foods
  * Household
  * Frozen Foods
  * Dairy
* Helps identify top-rated product categories

---

### 🏪 Outlet Analysis

#### 📏 Rating by Outlet Size

* Medium outlets contribute the highest ratings
* Followed by small and high-size outlets

#### 🏬 Rating by Outlet Type

* Supermarket Type 1 dominates in ratings and sales
* Grocery stores and other supermarket types follow

#### 📍 Item Visibility by Outlet Location

* Tier 3 locations show highest item visibility
* Tier 1 and Tier 2 follow

---

### ⚖️ Product Characteristics

#### 🥗 Item Weight by Fat Content

* Categories:

  * Low Fat
  * Regular
* Helps understand product distribution based on health preferences

---

### 📅 Establishment Insights

#### 🏢 Outlet Establishment by Location Tier

* Tier 3 outlets have the highest establishment count
* Tier 1 has the lowest

---

### 💰 Sales Analysis

#### 📊 Sales by Outlet Type

* Supermarket Type 1 leads in total sales
* Other outlet types contribute moderately

---

### 👁️ Visibility Metrics

* Total and average item visibility metrics
* Helps analyze product placement effectiveness

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Tool Used:** Power BI
* **Dataset:** Blinkit Grocery Dataset
* **Data Processing:** Power Query / Excel

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Supermarket Type 1** is the top-performing outlet type in both sales and ratings.
* **Tier 3 locations** show higher product visibility and outlet presence.
* **Medium-sized outlets** contribute significantly to overall performance.
* Products with **low fat content** show strong distribution trends.
* Certain product categories like snacks and dairy dominate in ratings.

---

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Open the `.pbix` file in Power BI Desktop.
2. Interact with charts to filter and drill down into data.
3. Analyze product categories, outlet performance, and trends.
4. Use insights to improve business strategies.
